Whole-body scope

Six health pillars. One quiet companion.

Built on workplace-health evidence — the 20-20-20 rule, 2→4 h/day standing guidance, micro-breaks and digital resilience practice — not gimmicks.

Eye health

20-20-20 breaks with a live countdown, blink reminders, and guided palming & figure-8 exercises with weekly adherence trends.

Movement

Sit/stand tracking toward the evidence-based 2→4 h/day standing goal, guided movement blocks, and a sit-less timeline of your day.

Posture & body

Discomfort check-ins map your sorest areas to targeted micro-stretches, plus posture resets and ergonomic guidance that adapts to your trends.

Mind & resilience

Mood & stress check-ins, an "I feel stressed" reset with box breathing and 5-4-3-2-1 grounding, gratitude journal and reframing practice.

Lifestyle

Hydration tracking with smart pacing, rotating nutrition nudges, an evening wind-down checklist and a weekly off-screen recovery plan.

Deep work

A Pomodoro focus timer that protects flow — non-critical reminders pause automatically while you're in a session, and resume when you're done.

AI coach — fully private

LiquidAI's LFM2.5-230M running on your GPU via WebGPU. It knows your real day and answers warmly — without a single byte going to the cloud.

Reminders that respect you

One nudge at a time via an always-on-top popup that never steals focus. One click pauses everything for an hour when you need silence.

A score you can act on

A composite health score across all six pillars with streaks, weekly summaries and 7-day trends — plus deterministic insight cards telling you the one thing to do next.

On-device intelligence

A 230M-parameter coach that lives on your GPU

Tiny local models can't do everything — feed them raw data and they turn into robotic parsers. So WellBeing splits the work: a JavaScript orchestrator computes the facts from your real day deterministically, and the LLM adds one warm, conversational line. Fast, honest, and private by construction.

  • Zero cloud. The model downloads once (~210 MB) and everything runs locally via WebGPU.
  • Knows your day. Answers combine your live stats — breaks, standing time, stress — with practical advice.
  • Graceful everywhere. No WebGPU? A rich rule-based coach takes over automatically.
